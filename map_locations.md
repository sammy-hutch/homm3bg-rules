  ## Gem Token: Ancient Relic
  **Gem Tokens** represent rare ancient relics found throughout the game world. They are vital for scoring and activating powerful abilities.
  
  ### Acquisition
  *   Can be found by successfully completing `Quest Cards`.
  *   Rarely obtained from specific `Event Cards`.

  ### Usage & Scoring
  Each Gem Token is worth **2 Victory Points** at the end of the game. Some `Mythic Cards` require 3 Gem Tokens to activate their ultimate power.

<div style="flex: 1;">
  ## Basic Movement Rules
  Players move their Pawns across the game board.
  
  ### Movement Allowance
  Each player has a base movement allowance of **4 spaces** per turn. This can be modified by `Terrain Effects` or `Ability Cards`.

  ### Terrain Effects
  *   **Forest:** Costs 2 movement points to enter.
  *   **Mountain:** Costs 3 movement points to enter; impassable without a `Climbing Gear` item.
  *   **River:** Can only be crossed at `Bridge Hexes`.
</div>
